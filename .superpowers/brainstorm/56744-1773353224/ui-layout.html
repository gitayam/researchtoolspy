📊 Matrix Tab

The grid shown above. Click cells to score, inline editing, add/remove/reorder rows & columns. Scorer toggle for Delphi mode.

⚖️ Weights Tab

Manual sliders, equal distribution button, or AHP pairwise wizard. Shows weight bar chart and consistency ratio (if AHP).

🏆 Results Tab

Ranked bar chart, radar/spider chart overlay for top alternatives, dominance matrix, score breakdown waterfall chart.

🌡️ Sensitivity Tab

Tornado diagram showing which weights matter most. Interactive sliders to see rank changes in real-time. Break-even analysis.

🤝 Consensus Tab

Delphi view — heatmap of scorer agreement, Kendall's W coefficient, per-cell IQR, highlight disagreement hotspots, round history.

🤖 AI Insights Tab

AI-generated summary, criteria suggestions, "challenge my analysis" mode, sensitivity narrative, blind spot detection.
